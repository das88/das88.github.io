My Pomegranate Blueberry Flavor is greyed out – what is going on?

We have learned of a manufacturing issue that affects Bevi's newest flavor, Pomegranate Blueberry + Electrolytes.  As a precaution to all of our customers, we have disabled this flavor from being dispensed from all machines that are online. We are asking our partners to replace it with another flavor and have stopped all shipments of this flavor until we complete testing. We have submitted samples for evaluation at an accredited lab, and results have confirmed that there was no health risk associated with the product. We truly apologize for any inconvenience  or disruption this may cause to your business . If you have any questions, please reach out to our Customer Service team at support@bevi.co. What type of ingredients does Bevi use in its flavors?

All Bevi flavors are made of natural ingredients. “Natural,” in context of flavors means that the flavor originated from a natural source and therefore no synthetic raw materials are a part of the formula. 

Are Bevi flavors made from fruit?

Bevi flavors are made with natural ingredients which are derived from natural sources. Bevi flavors do not contain juice. 

Does Bevi use anything in addition to the natural ingredients?

Bevi flavors never contain artificial ingredients.

What is in Bevi’s sweetened flavors?

Bevi’s sweetened flavors are sweetened with either Stevia (0 calories) or with Sugar (containing calories). We never use any high fructose corn syrup in our flavors.

Does Bevi measure pH?

Bevi measures flavor pH; Bevi flavors have very low levels of citric acid with pH ~3.5 – 4. There is less than a tenth of a gram of citric acid in a 12 oz. drink so the levels are very low. The citric acid used is from naturally derived, GMO free raw materials.
